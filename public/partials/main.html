Instructions

1. Pick a Machine

To begin, click a machine from the selection above.

2. Fill in the Parts

Move the part names from the given list onto the colored rings via drag and drop.

2a. Drag the part name from the list onto a ring.

2b. Replace used parts.

2c. Rearrange used parts.

2d. Sometimes there are hints.

If available, clicking the question mark in a circle will toggle the hint.

3. Get Your Results

Once you are satisfied with your decisions, click Submit at the bottom.

3a. You can get the results one section at a time

3b. And/Or get the total.

3c. If you want to retry, you can make modifications or click Reset.
